Our Mission

Welcome to the Khmer Food & Farmers Market Finder! Our mission is to connect locals and tourists with organic, fresh, and locally produced foods in Cambodia. By providing an easy way to discover and visit farmers' markets, we aim to support local farmers, promote healthy eating, and celebrate Cambodian culture through food.

What We Offer

We provide a comprehensive directory of farmers' markets across Cambodia, featuring detailed information about each market, the products they offer, and their locations. Our interactive map helps you find nearby markets and plan your visit. Whether you're looking for organic vegetables, fresh fruits, or traditional Cambodian products, we have you covered.

Our Vision

We envision a thriving community where people come together to support local farmers and enjoy fresh, healthy, and sustainable food. By fostering a deeper connection between people and the land, we aim to inspire a healthier lifestyle and a stronger economy in Cambodia.

Join Us

Interested in being a part of our community? Whether you’re a vendor or a visitor, we invite you to explore the markets, meet the farmers, and enjoy the rich flavors of Cambodia. Together, we can create a sustainable future for Cambodia's food system!
